Step-by-step guide

How to add a video

Upload once to Doodstream, copy one link, and it plays on your site. Follow the 6 steps below.

1

Open Doodstream and log in

Go to Doodstream and sign in (or create a free account).

doodstream.com
Log in Sign up
2

Click Upload

Top menu → Upload. Choose one:

Upload Video = pick a file from your PC.
Remote Upload = paste a video URL, Doodstream grabs it.

doodstream.com/upload
⬆ Upload Video 🔗 Remote Upload
Wait until the video shows Ready / Processed. Big files take a few minutes.
3

Open the video, find Embed

Click your uploaded video. Look for the Embed tab (or Share button).

doodstream.com/d/yourvideo
Video linkEmbed

Copy this URL:

https://dood.to/e/abc123xyz
⚠️ Must be the /e/ link (the embed one). NOT the normal watch page. /e/ = plays inside your site.
4

Copy title, category, embed link

You now have 3 things to add:

Title — what shows on the card
Category — amateur / solo / couples / asian / featured
Embed link — the /e/ URL from step 3

5

Add it to videos.json

Easiest: paste the 3 things to Claude and it adds + publishes for you.

Manual way — edit on GitHub: open videos.json → pencil ✏️ → add this block inside the list:

{
  "id": "v3",
  "title": "My Video Title",
  "category": "amateur",
  "creator": "Creator Name",
  "thumb": "",
  "embed": "https://dood.to/e/abc123xyz",
  "added": "2026-07-22"
}
Put a comma , between blocks. No comma after the last one. One bad comma breaks the gallery.
6

Publish — it goes live

Save / Commit on GitHub. GitHub rebuilds in ~2 minutes.

Open the Watch page → your video shows as a card → click → plays in a popup.
