DMCA Takedown Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026. This page is a starter template and should be reviewed by a qualified lawyer.

Submit a takedown notice

Send notices to: dmca@example.com. Replace this with your real DMCA contact before launch.

Your notice should include:

  • Your legal name and contact information.
  • The copyrighted work you claim is infringed.
  • The exact URL of the allegedly infringing content.
  • A good-faith statement that the use is not authorized.
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that your notice is accurate.
  • Your physical or electronic signature.

Counter-notices

If you believe content was removed by mistake, send a counter-notice with your contact information, removed URL, consent to jurisdiction, and signature.

Repeat infringers

Accounts that repeatedly submit infringing content may be terminated.

Important

This policy supports lawful takedown handling. It is not a shield for uploading content without rights or permission.
